Hello!

I'm Jim Basio.

—a Product Designer originally hailing from The North (aka Sweden).

You can find some of my design work on Dribbble / thoughts on Twitter / code on Github / photography on Instagram & Unsplash / and resume on LinkedIn.

With 10+ years of hands-on experience, I've most recently worked as a Principal Product Designer for Vistaprint. Former Designer for Cozy, Contactually, Nclud, and Viget.

Not a stranger to big brands, I've worked on projects for National Geographic, PBS, DIRECTV, Discovery Channel, Rosetta Stone, Mattel, Nasdaq and more.

In my spare time, I'm an avid traveler, bicyclist, footballer, photographer, and environmentalist. I'm also Co-Owner and Creative Director for Fútbolgods and Outrun Garage. Lastly, I play drums in a metal band and in my own solo music project LTWOI [two thumbs up].
